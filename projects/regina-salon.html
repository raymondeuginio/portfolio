Regina Salon Management System

Laravel | MySQL | TailwindCSS | Livewire

Overview

Regina Salon needed a central platform to coordinate appointments, resources, and stylist workloads. I designed a management system that automates booking flows, balances staff availability, and gives the owner visibility into day-to-day metrics.

Tech Stack

Laravel Livewire MySQL TailwindCSS Alpine.js

Features

  • Calendar-based appointment scheduler with automatic clash detection.
  • Inventory module tracking product usage and low-stock alerts.
  • Role-aware dashboard for owner, receptionist, and stylist access.
  • Automated SMS reminders for clients based on appointment status.

Screenshots

Outcome

The system reduced manual scheduling errors by 60% and allowed the team to forecast peak periods. It also sharpened my ability to structure modular Laravel components and introduce live validation patterns.
