Regina Salon & Spa

Laravel 12 · Filament · Tailwind CSS · Alpine.js

Overview

Regina Salon & Spa is a Laravel 12 application that digitises the end-to-end booking experience for a premium beauty salon. The site showcases curated services, highlights the salon brand, and lets registered customers assemble multi-service appointments, assign their preferred stylists, and confirm their visit online through a calendar-based booking journey.

Tech Stack

Laravel 12 PHP ^8.2 Alpine.js Vue 3 Tailwind CSS 4 MySQL

Features

  • Marketing landing page with brand story, services highlights, and contact details tailored to Regina Salon.
  • Service catalogue grouped by category, complete with pricing, durations, and a smart summary panel that tracks the customers’ selections in real time.
  • Guided, calendar-based booking flow that validates stylist availability, prevents schedule conflicts, and notifies the team once confirmed.
  • Authentication & profile management powered by Laravel Breeze so returning customers can reuse their stored contact details.
  • Seeded demo data for stores, categories, services, staff, and admin accounts to help you explore the experience instantly.

Outcome

Delivering Regina Salon & Spa sharpened my approach to Laravel domain design and front-of-house booking UX. The release balanced brand storytelling with operational tooling so the salon team can scale services without compromising personal touch.
