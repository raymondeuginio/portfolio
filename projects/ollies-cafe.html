Ollie’s Café QR Self-Order System

Laravel | Inertia.js | TailwindCSS | MySQL

Overview

Ollie’s Café sought to modernize their ordering experience. I introduced a QR-based ordering system allowing guests to browse the menu, customize items, and pay from their phones. The kitchen receives prioritized tickets while the cashier monitors payments in a single dashboard.

Tech Stack

Laravel Inertia.js Vue 3 TailwindCSS MySQL

Features

  • QR code table pairing with secure session handling.
  • Real-time order status updates for both baristas and guests.
  • Menu CMS for seasonal drinks with photo and pricing controls.
  • Sales analytics dashboard for daily and weekly performance.

Screenshots

Outcome

The café processed 35% more orders during peak hours without adding new staff. I gained experience optimizing Laravel queues for payment webhooks and deploying SSR-driven frontends with Inertia.
